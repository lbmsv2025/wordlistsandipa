About This Website

Improve Pronunciation with IPA and Word Practice

A learning companion designed to help language learners build clearer, more confident, and more accurate English pronunciation.

For many language learners, pronunciation is a difficult and complex skill to master. Clear and accurate pronunciation is essential to any profession, whether you're in the early years of your course or gearing up for your final teaching practicum.

Our website/app is tailored to address the modeling deficit that can take place online or in remote learning. By offering an organised, interactive approach to learning word lists and the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA), this tool serves as a virtual companion to help you enhance your intonation patterns and clarity.

Learning Focus

IPA Learn phonetic symbols and sounds clearly.
Practice Listen, repeat, and compare pronunciation.
Confidence Prepare for speaking and teaching situations.
🎧

Breaking down Assessment Barriers

This interactive guide provides visual and aural clues to correct pronunciation and intonation, which are best learned through repetitive practice rather than reading list entries in text.

🗣️

Overcoming Performance Anxiety

Through the ability to practice in a controlled environment, rather than live in front of others, a student's self-confidence for teaching practices or readiness to teach can be boosted.

Instant Feedback

🔤 English Sound Classification Guide

  • This guide categorizes English sounds into vowels and consonants based on how they are produced.
  • Vowels are produced with free airflow, while consonants involve some obstruction.
Category Sounds Meaning / How It Works
