Improve Pronunciation with IPA and Word Practice

A learning companion designed to help language learners build clearer, more confident, and more accurate English pronunciation.

For many language learners, pronunciation is a difficult and complex skill to master. Clear and accurate pronunciation is essential to any profession, whether you're in the early years of your course or gearing up for your final teaching practicum.

By offering an organised, interactive approach to learning word lists and the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA), this tool serves as a virtual companion to help you enhance your intonation patterns and clarity.

What are Minimal Pairs?

Minimal pairs are two words that sound almost the same, but have one different sound.

Because of that small sound difference, the meaning of the word also changes. Practicing minimal pairs helps learners hear and pronounce English sounds more clearly.

For example, ship and sheep are minimal pairs. They are different because of the vowel sound: /ɪ/ in ship and /iː/ in sheep.
