For many language learners, pronunciation is a difficult and complex skill to master. Clear and accurate pronunciation is essential to any profession, whether you're in the early years of your course or gearing up for your final teaching practicum.

Our website is tailored to address the modeling deficit that can take place online or in remote learning. By offering an organised, interactive approach to learning word lists and the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA), this tool is a virtual companion to help you enhance your intonation patterns and clarity.  


Why This Tool Matters

Breaking down Assessment Barriers: This interactive guide provides visual and aural clues to correct pronunciation and intonation, which are best learned through repetitive practice, rather than reading list entries in text.  
Overcoming Performance Anxiety: Through the ability to practice in a controlled environment, rather than live in front of others, a student's self-confidence for teaching practices - or readiness to teach - can be boosted.  
Instant Feedback: The application overcomes the absence of monitoring by letting you compare your pronunciation against phonetic transcriptions.  

Key Features

IPA Sound Table: Understand the components of English in this detailed examination of phonetic symbols.
Word Lists: Work on selected vocabulary to overcome common pronunciation issues.
Easy Access: Built for the modern Pre-Service Teacher, this resource is available whenever you want it, to help you to develop the high-fidelity skills you need as a professional.
